Who We Are

About Us

Wireless Services Asia (WSA) was founded in 1999 as a mobile value-added services (VAS) provider. In 2008, the company transformed into a carrier-grade services organization and emerged as one of the Philippines' leading resellers of integrated technology and business solutions for the telecommunications industry.

In 2017, WSA expanded beyond the telco space with the establishment of its Enterprise Business Unit, enabling the company to deliver customized software and technology solutions to non-telecommunications enterprises across multiple industries.

Today, WSA collaborates with world-class technology partners to provide advanced networking and application solutions, supporting communications service providers and enterprise clients in achieving greater efficiency, scalability, and innovation across their vertical markets.
