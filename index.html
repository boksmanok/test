About WSA

Wireless Services Asia (WSA) was formed in 1999 as a mobile VAS provider. As a result of a management buy-out in 2008, the company has transformed itself to be a carrier grade services company and one of the leading Resellers and Systems Integrators of technology and business solutions in the Philippines. WSA partners with world-class technology companies in providing Network and Application solutions to Telecom Services Providers and Enterprise clients.
