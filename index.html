Who We Are

Find Out More About Us

Wireless Services Asia (WSA) was formed in 1999 as a mobile VAS provider. As a result of a management buy-out in 2008, the company has transformed itself to be a carrier grade services company and one of the leading Resellers and Systems Integrators of technology and business solutions in the Philippines.



WSA partners with world-class technology companies in providing Network and Application solutions to Telecom Services Providers and Enterprise clients.

  • Telecommunications Solutions

    - Core Network Signaling (SS7, SIP, Diameter) Solutions
    - Customer Value Management, Cognitive Loyalty Management, AdMedia
    - DNS, Browsing Security, Parental Control, In-Browser Communications
    - Intelligent Demarcation Devices
    - HP Enterprise Communications & Media Solutions
    - Subscriber Revenue Service to monetize traditional cost center assets
    - Anti-spam and traffic control
    - Fleet Management System based on local landmarks instead of GPS coordinates
    - Internet of Things (IoT)
    - Operational Intelligence

Enterprises need to keep up with the disruption caused by emerging technologies and changing customer behavior. The high rate of adoption of mobile devices in the workplace have required the need for better security and device management while customers have become more selective in their choice of products due to easily available information. Customers are also expecting a higher level of customer experience – more personalized product/service offers, better logistics support.

    - Internet of Things (IoT) & Industrial Data
    - Security and Compliance
    - Anti-spam and traffic control
    - MarkLogic Database for Integrating Data from Silos
